About Omnitrix Technologies

Since 2015, Omnitrix Technologies has been a leading provider of Research and Development (R&D) and PCB development services in Pune and Maharashtra, India. We specialize in both small-scale and high-volume production as quick-turn manufacturers. Omnitrix Technologies serves a diverse range of industries that demand high-quality and reliable printed circuit boards delivered on time. Our customers, spanning sectors such as military, defense, medical, and other critical applications, trust us for the exceptional PCBs we supply.
